# Strategy Frameworks

Reference document containing strategic frameworks for campaign development.

## Positioning Frameworks

### 1. Positioning Statement Template

```
For [target audience] who [statement of need or opportunity],
[Brand] is a [product category] that [key benefit].
Unlike [main competitor],
[Brand] [key differentiator].
```

## KPI Frameworks

### 1. Marketing KPI Hierarchy

```
BUSINESS GOAL
    │
    └── Marketing Objective
            │
            ├── Primary KPI (1)
            │   └── Target: [value]
            │
            └── Secondary KPIs (2-3)
                ├── KPI 2: Target
                └── KPI 3: Target
```

### 2. Channel-Specific KPIs

| Channel | Primary KPI | Secondary KPIs |
|---------|-------------|----------------|
| SEO | Organic Traffic | Rankings, CTR |
| Content | Engagement | Time on page, Shares |
| Email | Open Rate | CTR, Conversions |
| Social | Engagement | Followers, Reach |
| PPC | ROAS | CTR, CPC |
