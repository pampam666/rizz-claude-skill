# Keyword Gap Methodology

## Overview

This document outlines the methodology for identifying and exploiting keyword opportunities that competitors are missing.

 The renewable energy market in Indonesia.

---

## What is Keyword Gap Analysis?

Keyword gap analysis identifies high-value search terms that:
1. Have significant search volume (user demand exists)
2. Have low to moderate competition (easier to rank for)
3. Are NOT currently being targeted by the competitor

---

## Methodology Steps

### Step 1: Compile Competitor Keyword Data

Gather the competitor's current keyword rankings from:
- Ahrefs "Organic Keywords" report
- Semrush "Organic Research" report
- Google Search Console (if competitor access available)

**Data to Extract:**
| Keyword | Current Position | Search Volume | Keyword Difficulty |
|---------|------------------|---------------|-------------------|
| [keyword] | [1-100] | [monthly searches] | [0-100 KD score] |

### Step 2: Identify Market Keywords
Generate a comprehensive list of industry-relevant keywords:

**Renewable Energy Core Keywords:**
```

```

### Step 3: Gap Identification Matrix
Create a comparison matrix:

| Keyword | Search Volume | Competitor Position | Gap Score |
|---------|---------------|--------------------| ---------|
| [keyword] | [volume] | [position or "Not Ranking"] | [volume × difficulty / position] |

**Gap Score Formula:**
```
Gap Score = (Search Volume × Keyword Difficulty) / Competitor Position
```
- Higher score = better opportunity
- Position 1-10: Divisor = 1-10
- Position 11-20: Divisor = 11-20
- Not Ranking: Divisor = 100 (maximum opportunity)

### Step 4: Prioritization Framework
Rank opportunities by:

**Priority 1 - Quick Wins:**
- High volume (>1000/month)
- Low difficulty (<40 KD)
- Not ranking by competitor

**Priority 2 - Medium Term:**
- Medium volume (500-1000/month)
- Medium difficulty (40-60 KD)
- Position 21-50 by competitor

**Priority 3 - Long Term:**
- High volume (>2000/month)
- High difficulty (>60 KD)
- Any competitor position

## Gap Categories

### 1. Content Gaps
Keywords where competitor has no content at all:
- **Indicator**: Competitor doesn't rank for obvious industry terms
- **Action**: Create comprehensive content immediately
- **Timeline**: 2-4 weeks to rank

### 2. Ranking Gaps
Keywords where competitor ranks but poorly (position 11+):
- **Indicator**: Competitor has content but low authority
- **Action**: Create better content + build backlinks
- **Timeline**: 1-3 months to outrank

### 3. Intent Gaps
Keywords where competitor focuses on wrong intent:
- **Example**: Competitor ranks for "panel surya" (informational) but not "harga panel surya" (transactional)
- **Action**: Create intent-specific landing pages
- **Timeline**: 2-4 weeks
