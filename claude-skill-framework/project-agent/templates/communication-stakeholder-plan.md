## 5. Communication Channels

### 5.1 Email Guidelines
| Purpose | Structure | Tips |
|---------|-----------|------|
| Status updates | Clear subject, project name, brief bullet points | Keep under 200 words |
| Meeting requests | Date, time, location, agenda | Send 48 hours in advance |
| Document sharing | Brief description, link | Use clear file naming |
| Issue escalation | Clear problem, impact, requested action | Be specific about needs |

### 5.2 Instant Messaging Guidelines
| Platform | Use Case | Response Time |
|---------|---------|---------------|
| Slack/Teams | Quick questions | Within 2 hours |
| Slack/Teams | Urgent issues | Within 1 hour |
| WhatsApp | Time-sensitive | Within 30 minutes |

### 5.3 Document Sharing Protocol
| Document Type | Naming Convention | Location | Access |
|---------------|-------------------|----------|--------|
| Reports | YYYY-MM-DD_[Report Name]_v[Version] | Shared Drive | Team |
| Plans | [Project Name]_[Plan Type]_v[Version] | Shared Drive | Team + Supervisor |
| Templates | [Template Name]_Template | Templates folder | Team |
| Meeting Notes | YYYY-MM-DD_[Meeting Type]_Notes | Shared Drive | Team |

## 6. Escalation Procedures

### 6.1 Issue Escalation Matrix
| Severity | Response Time | Escalation Path | Decision Maker |
|----------|---------------|-----------------|----------------|
| Low | 24-48 hours | Team Lead resolves | Team Lead |
| Medium | 8-24 hours | PM involvement | Project Manager |
| High | 2-8 hours | Supervisor involvement | Supervisor |
| Critical | Immediate | Management involvement | Management |

### 6.2 Escalation Triggers
| Trigger | Description | Response |
|--------|-------------|----------|
| Scope change | Any addition to project scope | PM review + Supervisor approval |
| Budget overrun | >10% budget variance | PM review + re-approval |
| Timeline slip | >3 days delay on PM review + recovery plan |
| Resource conflict | Team availability issues | PM review + resource reallocation |
| Quality issue | Major deliverable defects | PM review + QA escalation |
